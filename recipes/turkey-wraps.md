# Turkey Wraps

A fast, no-cook lunch: turkey, Swiss, and avocado wrapped in lettuce or a whole grain tortilla.

## Ingredients

- Turkey breast
- Swiss cheese
- Avocado, sliced
- Large lettuce leaves
- Whole grain tortillas (optional)
- Drizzle of light mayo

## Steps

1. Using a lettuce leaf or tortilla as the base, layer on turkey breast, cheese, sliced avocado, and a drizzle of mayo. Wrap and serve.
