# Earl Grey Tea

A quick, no-fuss cup. Placeholder until we drop in your saved tea recipe.

## Ingredients

- 1 Earl Grey tea bag (or 1 tsp loose leaf)
- 8 oz fresh water, just off the boil (~200°F)
- Optional: milk, honey, or a lemon slice

## Steps

1. Heat water to just below boiling.
2. Pour over the tea bag in your cup.
3. Steep 3–4 minutes. Longer gets bitter.
4. Remove the bag. Add milk, honey, or lemon to taste.
