# Eggnog Pie

A no-bake eggnog cream pie on a cinnamon graham crust, with a homemade eggnog recipe included.

## Ingredients

**Graham Cracker Crust**

- 1 1/2 cups finely crushed graham cracker crumbs
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon

**Homemade Eggnog**

- 5 eggs, yolks and whites separated
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 4 cloves
- Pinch of cinnamon
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Splash of vanilla

**Eggnog Pie**

- 2 cups cold eggnog
- 5.1 oz box vanilla pudding
- 1 cup whipped cream, folded in
- Splash of dark spiced rum

## Steps

**Graham Cracker Crust**

1. Grease a 9-inch glass pie pan and set aside.
2. Mix all crust ingredients and press evenly into the pan.

**Homemade Eggnog**

1. Whisk together the 5 egg yolks and 1 cup sugar. Set aside.
2. Add the whole milk, heavy cream, cloves, and a pinch of cinnamon to a pot. Bring to scalding, not a boil.
3. Slowly whisk the milk mixture into the egg yolks and sugar, tempering.
4. Return to the pot over low heat and whisk until thick.
5. Strain the mixture.
6. Fluff the egg whites with a hand mixer, adding a pinch of sugar.
7. Fold the egg whites into the milk mixture, adding a pinch of nutmeg and some vanilla.
8. Let rest in the fridge for at least four hours.

**Eggnog Pie**

1. Mix together the 2 cups cold eggnog and the box of vanilla pudding, making sure there are no clumps.
2. Beat together 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream, sugar, and a splash of vanilla.
3. Fold together the whipped cream and eggnog mixture. Add a splash of dark spiced rum.
4. Pour over the graham cracker crust, spreading evenly. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
