# Chocolate Treats

No-bake chocolate graham cookies dipped in melted chocolate and finished with sprinkles.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups crushed graham crackers
- 4 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1/4 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup milk
- Semi-sweet chocolate chips
- Chocolate sprinkles

## Steps

1. Crush the graham crackers in a food processor.
2. Add the cocoa powder and melted butter.
3. Gradually add the milk until you get a dough-like consistency.
4. Roll into a log shape to slice later. Leave in the fridge for a few hours.
5. Remove from the fridge and slice into cookie-like shapes.
6. Melt the chocolate chips and dip the slices into the melted chocolate.
7. Garnish with chocolate sprinkles.
