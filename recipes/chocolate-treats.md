# Chocolate Treats

No-bake chocolate graham cookies dipped in melted chocolate and finished with sprinkles. Fun to make and easy to share.

## Ingredients

- 3 cups graham crackers, crushed
- 4 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 3/4 cup milk
- Semisweet chocolate chips
- Chocolate sprinkles

## Steps

1. Crush the graham crackers in a food processor.
2. Add the cocoa powder and melted butter.
3. Gradually add the milk until the mixture reaches a dough-like consistency.
4. Roll into a log, then refrigerate for a few hours.
5. Remove from the fridge and slice into cookie-shaped rounds.
6. Melt the chocolate chips and dip each slice into the melted chocolate.
7. Garnish with chocolate sprinkles and serve.
