# Creamy Tomato Pasta Sauce

A silky, restaurant-style tomato sauce made with butter, garlic, and a splash of heavy cream, finished with Parmesan and fresh parsley. Rich and velvety, it coats every bite of pasta in comforting flavor.

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces penne pasta
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic
- 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tsp salt (or to taste)
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over medium-high heat. Add pasta and cook until al dente, about 10 minutes or according to package directions. Drain the pasta well and set aside.
2. Heat butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat until bubbly hot, about 1 minute. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Pour in tomato sauce and tomato paste. Stir and bring it to a simmer, about 4-5 minutes.
4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in heavy cream and parmesan cheese. Cook until thickened to a desired consistency, about 3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add in the cooked pasta and toss well to coat evenly.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.

## Notes
- Add a protein such as meatballs or ground Italian sausage for a heartier meal.
- Garlic knots pair well with this dish.
