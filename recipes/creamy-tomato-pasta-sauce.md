# Creamy Tomato Pasta Sauce

A silky, restaurant-style tomato sauce made with butter, garlic, and a splash of heavy cream, finished with Parmesan and fresh parsley. Rich and velvety, it coats every bite of pasta.

## Ingredients

- 8 oz penne pasta
- 3 tbsp butter
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
- 1/4 tsp black pepper
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley

## Steps

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over medium-high heat. Add the pasta and cook until al dente, about 10 minutes or according to the package directions. Drain well and set aside.
2. Heat the butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat until bubbly, about 1 minute. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Pour in the tomato sauce and tomato paste. Stir and bring to a simmer, about 4–5 minutes.
4. Reduce the heat to low and stir in the heavy cream and Parmesan. Cook until thickened to your desired consistency, about 3 minutes, then season with salt and pepper.
5. Add the cooked pasta and toss well to coat evenly.
6. Garnish with parsley and serve warm.

## Notes

- Add a protein such as meatballs or ground Italian sausage for a heartier meal.
- Garlic knots pair well with this dish.
