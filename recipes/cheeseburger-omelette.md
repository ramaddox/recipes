# Cheeseburger Omelette

A protein-packed breakfast twist on the classic cheeseburger: seasoned ground beef folded into fluffy egg whites with onion and spinach, finished with a drizzle of sriracha. Hearty and savory.

## Ingredients

- 4 oz ground beef
- 1 cup egg whites
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 cup spinach
- Salt, black pepper, paprika, and garlic powder, to taste
- Sriracha, to serve

## Steps

1. Brown the ground beef with the salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder, then push it to one half of the pan.
2. Add the egg whites, onion, and spinach to the other half of the pan.
3. Cover and cook until the eggs are set.
4. Fold over, plate, and drizzle with sriracha to serve.
