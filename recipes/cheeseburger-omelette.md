# Cheeseburger Omelette

A protein-packed breakfast twist on the classic cheeseburger—seasoned ground beef folded into fluffy egg whites with onion and spinach, finished with a drizzle of sriracha. Hearty, savory, and ready to fuel your morning.

## Ingredients

- 4 oz ground beef
- Salt, pepper, paprika and garlic powder
- 1 cup egg whites
- 1 medium onion chopped
- 1 cup spinach

## Steps

1. Brown ground beef with seasonings, push to 1/2 of pan.
2. Add egg whites, onion and spinach to other half of pan
3. Cover and cook until eggs cook
4. Fold over and plate, drizzle with sriracha
