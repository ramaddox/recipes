# Roast Beef & Bow Tie Pasta w/ Broccoli & Parmesan

Slow-cooked shredded chuck roast tossed with farfalle, broccoli, and parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups uncooked farfalle pasta
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 2-3 lb chuck roast
- 2 cups beef broth
- 1 1/2 cups broccoli
- Salt and ground black pepper
- Garlic salt
- Thyme
- 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
- 1 tbsp green onions, for garnish

## Steps

1. Pat the roast dry with paper towels.
2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic salt, and thyme.
3. Pour the beef broth around the sides of the roast in a crockpot.
4. Cover and cook on high for 6 hours or low for 8 hours.
5. Remove the roast and let it rest for 15 minutes.
6. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
7. While the pasta cooks, shred the roast beef.
8. Heat the olive oil in a pan until hot. Add the shredded beef and broccoli and heat through.
9. Drain the pasta and add to the beef mixture. Stir in salt, pepper, and parmesan. Garnish with green onions.
