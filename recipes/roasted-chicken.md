# Roasted Chicken

A butter-basted whole chicken rubbed with herbs and spices and roasted in an oven bag until juicy and tender.

## Ingredients

- 1 young chicken
- 1 1/2 sticks butter, room temperature
- 1 stick butter, cold, cut into 6–8 slices
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/2 onion
- Rosemary sprigs
- Celery seed
- Carrots
- Chicken broth

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Inject the chicken using your injection recipe.
3. Make a slit at the top of each breast and insert the cold butter slices.
4. Combine the room-temperature butter with the paprika, onion powder, garlic powder, thyme, pepper, cayenne, and salt. Rub all over the chicken.
5. Stuff the cavity with the onion, rosemary sprigs, celery seed, and carrot.
6. Place the bird in an oven bag and pour in chicken broth to cover the bottom of the pan.
7. Roast for 2 hours 10 minutes.
8. Check for an internal temperature of 180°F, then rest before serving.
