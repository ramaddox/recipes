# Roasted Chicken

A butter-basted, herb-and-spice roasted whole chicken cooked in an oven bag until juicy.

## Ingredients

- 1 young chicken
- 1 1/2 sticks butter, room temperature
- 1 stick butter, cold, cut into 6-8 slices
- 1 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp garlic
- 1/2 tsp thyme
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp salt
- Onion
- Rosemary sprigs
- Celery seed
- Carrots
- Chicken broth

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Inject the chicken using your injection recipe.
3. Make a slit at the top of each breast and insert the cold butter slices.
4. Combine the room-temp butter and spices. Rub all over the chicken.
5. Stuff the chicken with 1/2 an onion, thyme, rosemary sprigs, celery seed, and carrot.
6. Place the bird into an oven bag and pour chicken broth in to cover the bottom of the pan/bag.
7. Roast for 2 hours, 10 minutes.
8. Check for an internal temperature of 180F.
