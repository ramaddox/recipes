# Smothered Steak

Cubed steak browned and baked in a rich onion gravy. Comfort food best served over mashed potatoes.

## Ingredients

- Cubed steak
- Seasoning of choice
- Olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 yellow onion, sliced
- 1 cup all-purpose flour, divided
- 1 tsp Better Than Bouillon (beef base)
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup water

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Rub both sides of the steak with olive oil and the seasoning of your choice.
3. Combine 1/2 cup of the flour and the garlic powder in a bowl, then coat each side of the steak.
4. Heat 1/3 cup oil in a large skillet over medium heat until hot. Cook the steak in batches until browned, 3–4 minutes per side. Remove and repeat until all pieces are done.
5. Add the onion slices to the skillet and sauté 2 minutes, then place the onions atop the steak.
6. Stir together the water, beef base, and Worcestershire sauce in the skillet until it simmers. Add the remaining 1/2 cup flour and stir briskly until bubbly, 2–3 minutes.
7. Stir in the milk and keep stirring until the mixture bubbles again. Pour the gravy over the steak and onions.
8. Bake, covered with foil, for 45–60 minutes.
9. Serve with mashed potatoes and a veggie of choice.
