# Smothered Steak

Cubed steak browned and baked in a rich onion gravy. Serve with mashed potatoes.

## Ingredients

- Cubed steak
- Seasoning of choice
- Olive oil
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 yellow onion, sliced
- 1 cup all-purpose flour, divided (1/2 and 1/2)
- 1 tsp Better than Bouillon (beef base)
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 cups milk
- 1 cup water

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Rub both sides of the steak with olive oil and the seasoning(s) of your choice.
3. Combine 1/2 cup flour and the garlic powder in a bowl. Coat each side of the steak.
4. Heat 1/3 cup oil in a large skillet over medium heat until hot. Cook the steak in batches until browned, 3-4 minutes per side. Remove and repeat until all pieces are done.
5. Add the onion slices to the skillet and saute 2 minutes. Place the onions atop the steak.
6. Stir together the water, beef base, and Worcestershire sauce in the skillet until it simmers. Add 1/2 cup flour and stir briskly until bubbly, 2-3 minutes.
7. Stir in the milk and keep stirring until the mixture bubbles again. Pour the gravy over the steak and onions.
8. Bake, covered with foil, for 45-60 minutes.
9. Serve with mashed potatoes and a veggie of choice.
