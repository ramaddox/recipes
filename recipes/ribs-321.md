# 3/2/1 Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone oven ribs using the 3-2-1 method: 3 hours uncovered, 2 hours wrapped, 1 hour sauced.

## Ingredients

- 1 rack of ribs
- Dijon mustard
- Rib rub
- Apple cider vinegar
- BBQ sauce of choice

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 225F.
2. Remove the membrane from the ribs.
3. Rub the rack with a light layer of Dijon mustard.
4. Dust with your desired rib rub and work it into the meat.
5. Place in the oven for 3 hours.
6. Note: spritz with apple cider vinegar occasionally wherever you see the ribs drying out. Place a pan of water at the bottom of the oven.
7. Remove from the oven, spray the rack with apple cider vinegar, wrap in foil, and return to the oven for 2 hours.
8. Remove the rack and lather in the sauce of your choice.
9. Return to the oven for 1 hour (high-sugar sauces for only 30-45 minutes).
10. Remove from the oven. Enjoy.
