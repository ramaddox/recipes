# Slow Roasted Beef Short Ribs

Boneless short ribs slow-roasted in a sweet-and-smoky homemade BBQ sauce.

## Ingredients

- Boneless beef short ribs
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 cups ketchup
- 2 tbsp liquid smoke
- 1/2 to 2/3 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup minced onion
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 3/4 cup BBQ sauce

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 300F.
2. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, combine the ketchup, liquid smoke, brown sugar, minced onion, garlic powder, and BBQ sauce. Cook until thick and bubbly, stirring occasionally.
3. Season the short ribs all over with salt and pepper and place in a glass baking dish. Pour about 1/2 cup of sauce over them. Cover the pan with foil and roast for 2 1/2 hours.
4. Remove the foil and gently flip the short ribs. Spoon more sauce over the top. Cook, uncovered, for an additional 30 minutes, until the meat is tender and browned.
5. Serve over a bed of mashed potatoes with roasted veggies.
