# Butternut Squash Dish

A roasted butternut squash and potato sheet-pan dish glazed with balsamic and maple, then tossed with wilted spinach, pistachios, and Parmesan. Sweet, savory, and comforting.

## Ingredients

- 1 butternut squash, cubed
- 1 lb red potatoes, cubed
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh sage, chopped
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- Olive oil
- Crushed red pepper
- Salt and black pepper, to taste
- 5 oz baby spinach
- 1/4 cup pistachios, chopped
- Parmesan cheese

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
2. Combine all the ingredients except the spinach, pistachios, and Parmesan, tossing to coat.
3. Spread on a sheet pan and roast for 35–40 minutes.
4. Add the baby spinach and toss until it wilts.
5. Add the pistachios and Parmesan, toss well, and serve.
