# Butternut Squash Dish

A roasted butternut squash and potato sheet-pan dish with balsamic and maple, tossed with spinach, pistachios, and parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 1 butternut squash, cubed
- 1 lb red potatoes
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp sage, chopped
- Crushed red pepper
- Olive oil
- 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- 2 tbsp maple syrup
- Salt and pepper
- 5 oz baby spinach
- 1/4 cup chopped pistachios
- Parmesan cheese

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 450F.
2. Combine all ingredients except the spinach, pistachios, and parmesan.
3. Spread on a sheet pan and roast for 35-40 minutes.
4. Add the 5 oz baby spinach and mix until it wilts.
5. Add the pistachios and parmesan and mix well.
6. Enjoy.
