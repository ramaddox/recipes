# Chicken Salad

A creamy Greek-yogurt chicken salad with dried cranberries and toasted pine nuts. Great on whole grain crackers.

## Ingredients

- 2-3 chicken breasts
- 2 celery stalks
- 1-2 green onions
- 3/4 cup dried cranberries
- 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp light mayo
- 1/4 tsp dry mustard powder
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Shred the chicken, celery, green onion, and pine nuts in a food processor.
2. Pour into a bowl and add the Greek yogurt, mayo, mustard powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix together. Serve with whole grain crackers.
