# Chicken Salad

A creamy Greek-yogurt chicken salad studded with dried cranberries and toasted pine nuts. Great piled on whole grain crackers.

## Ingredients

- 2–3 chicken breasts
- 2 celery stalks
- 1–2 green onions
- 3/4 cup dried cranberries
- 1/2 cup pine nuts, toasted
- 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tbsp light mayo
- 1/4 tsp dry mustard powder
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Pulse the chicken, celery, green onion, and pine nuts in a food processor until shredded.
2. Transfer to a bowl and add the cranberries, Greek yogurt, mayo, mustard powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Mix well and serve with whole grain crackers.
