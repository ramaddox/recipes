# Mashed Potatoes

Creamy Yukon gold mashed potatoes infused with garlic and rosemary, whipped with butter, whole milk, and a splash of heavy cream. Rich, smooth, and the perfect comforting side.

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs medium Yukon gold potatoes, scrubbed
- 1.25 tsp kosher salt
- 1/2 head of garlic, halved crosswise
- 1/2 tsp rosemary
- 3/4 cup whole milk
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
- ground pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Place potatoes in a large pot and pour in cold water to cover by 1”. Add a large handful of salt. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender, but not falling apart (30-35 minutes).
2. Drain potatoes, briefly rinse under cool running water to remove any excess starch.
3. Heat garlic, rosemary sprigs, milk and cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
4. Start mashing potatoes with butter and salt until butter is incorporated.
5. Strain reserved milk mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a large measuring glass. Pour into mashed potatoes 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until liquid is fully incorporated and mixture is smooth.
6. Serve topped with a few grinds of pepper and a pat or 2 of butter if desired.
