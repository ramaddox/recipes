# Mashed Potatoes

Creamy Yukon gold mashed potatoes infused with garlic and rosemary, then whipped with butter, whole milk, and a splash of heavy cream. Rich, smooth, and the perfect comforting side.

## Ingredients

- 2 lbs medium Yukon gold potatoes, scrubbed
- 1.25 tsp kosher salt, plus more for the water
- 1/2 head garlic, halved crosswise
- 1/2 tsp rosemary
- 3/4 cup whole milk
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
- Ground black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Place the potatoes in a large pot and add cold water to cover by 1 inch. Add a large handful of salt and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender but not falling apart, 30–35 minutes.
2. Drain the potatoes and briefly rinse under cool running water to remove excess starch.
3. Heat the garlic, rosemary, milk, and cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
4. Mash the potatoes with the butter and kosher salt until the butter is incorporated.
5. Strain the milk mixture through a fine-mesh sieve into a measuring glass. Pour into the potatoes 1/2 cup at a time, stirring until fully incorporated and smooth.
6. Serve topped with a few grinds of pepper and a pat or two of butter, if desired.
