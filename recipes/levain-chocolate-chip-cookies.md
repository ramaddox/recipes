# Levain Chocolate Chip Cookies

Thick, bakery-style chocolate chip cookies with crisp edges and gooey centers.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold unsalted butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 cup cake flour (measure while sifting)
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour (measure while sifting)
- 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 410F.
2. Mix the cold butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until fluffy.
3. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing in between.
4. Sift the all-purpose and cake flours together three times.
5. Mix the cornstarch, baking soda, salt, cake flour, and all-purpose flour into the mixture on low until just blended.
6. Add the chocolate chips and mix.
7. Refrigerate the dough for 15 minutes.
8. Scoop into loose, large balls and place on a baking sheet.
9. Bake up to 18 minutes.
10. Let cool for 30 minutes before eating.
