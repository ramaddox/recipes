# Green Bean Casserole

A comforting classic made easy—tender French-style green beans in a creamy sauce, topped with crispy French fried onions and baked until golden. Serve as a cozy side, or add shredded chicken to make it a full meal.

## Ingredients

- 1 can cream of broccoli
- 2 cans French style green beans
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 package french fried onions

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
2. Stir the cream of broccoli, milk, soy sauce, green beans (drained), and 1/2 of french fried onions. Season with salt and pepper.
3. If you want to make this a main dish - add two to three chicken tenders or 1 chicken breast, shredded, to mixture.
4. Bake for 25 minutes. Stir the mixture.
5. Add remaining french fried onions to top of casserole. Return to over for 5 minutes until onions are golden brown.
6. Enjoy.

## Notes
- Add a poultry protein, such as chicken or turkey, to take this from a side dish to a main dish.
