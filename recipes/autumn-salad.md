# Autumn Salad

A hearty autumn salad piled with roasted sweet potatoes, crispy spiced chickpeas, and caramelized brussels sprouts over massaged kale, finished with avocado, sunflower seeds, and a creamy Parmesan-tahini dressing. Cozy, filling, and packed with flavor.

## Ingredients

- 2 sweet potatoes
- Olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 2 tsp garlic powder (divided)
- 2 cans chickpeas (rinsed and dried)
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 lb brussel sprouts (shredded)
- 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 4 cloves garlic
- 24 oz shredded kale
- 2 avocados (diced, optional)
- 3/4 cup sunflower seeds
- Salt & pepper to taste

## Steps

1. Prep sweet potatoes: slice 2 sweet potatoes into thin wedges or quarters.  Toss with olive oil, paprika, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
2. Prep chickpeas: rinse and dry 2 cans of chickpeas.  Toss in olive oil, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
3. Roast: spread sweet potatoes and chickpeas on separate baking sheets.  Roast at 425 degrees for 30 minutes.
- Shred brussel sprouts: shred 1 lb brussel sprouts (or use pre-shredded).  Toss with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
- If oven space allows, add them to roast for 15 minutes.  If not, wait until chickpeas and sweet potatoes finish.
4. Make dressing: combine the following in a blender
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 4 cloves garlic
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Blend until smooth.
5. Massage kale: toss 24 oz shredded curly kale in a large bowl.  Add dressing and massage until kale softens (about 2-3 minutes)
6. Assemble: Add roasted sweet potatoes, brussel sprouts, chickpeas, 2 diced avocados, and 3/4 cup sunflower seeds to the kale
7. Toss and serve: mix everything together and enjoy!
