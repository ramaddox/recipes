# Autumn Salad

A hearty salad piled with roasted sweet potatoes, crispy spiced chickpeas, and caramelized Brussels sprouts over massaged kale, finished with avocado, sunflower seeds, and a creamy Parmesan-tahini dressing. Cozy, filling, and full of flavor.

## Ingredients

- 2 sweet potatoes, cut into thin wedges
- 2 cans chickpeas, rinsed and dried
- 1 lb Brussels sprouts, shredded
- 24 oz shredded curly kale
- 2 avocados, diced (optional)
- 3/4 cup sunflower seeds
- Olive oil
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 2 tsp garlic powder, divided
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

**For the dressing**

- 1 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1/2 cup tahini
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 4 cloves garlic
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
2. Toss the sweet potatoes with olive oil, the paprika, 1 tsp garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Toss the chickpeas separately with olive oil, the chili powder, the remaining 1 tsp garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread the sweet potatoes and chickpeas on separate baking sheets and roast for 30 minutes.
4. Toss the shredded Brussels sprouts with olive oil, salt, and pepper. If oven space allows, roast them for the final 15 minutes; otherwise roast after the sweet potatoes and chickpeas finish.
5. Make the dressing: combine all dressing ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
6. Add the kale to a large bowl, pour over the dressing, and massage until the kale softens, about 2–3 minutes.
7. Add the roasted sweet potatoes, chickpeas, and Brussels sprouts along with the avocado and sunflower seeds.
8. Toss everything together and serve.
