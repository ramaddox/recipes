# Shirataki Noodle Stir Fry

A quick, low-carb veggie stir fry with shirataki noodles in a savory soy-oyster sauce. Add shredded chicken for protein.

## Ingredients

**Stir Fry Sauce**

- 1 1/2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp sriracha

**Rest**

- 16 oz shirataki noodles
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, julienned
- 1 cup carrot shreds
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1 tbsp oil, for frying

## Steps

1. Mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and sriracha.
2. Drain and rinse the shirataki noodles in a colander under running water for a minute. Set aside to dry in the colander until ready to use.
3. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a non-stick wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the garlic and fry for a minute.
4. Add the red bell pepper and carrot. Saute for 3-4 minutes, until slightly softened.
5. Add the cabbage and cook another 2-3 minutes, until the veggies are tender.
6. Add the noodles along with the stir-fry sauce. Toss to coat and fry until the noodles are hot, then remove from heat. Season as needed.

## Notes

- Optional: add shredded chicken for protein.
