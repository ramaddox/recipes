# Shirataki Noodle Stir Fry

A quick, low-carb veggie stir fry with shirataki noodles in a savory soy-oyster sauce. Add shredded chicken for extra protein.

## Ingredients

**For the stir-fry sauce**

- 1 1/2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp sriracha

**For the stir fry**

- 16 oz shirataki noodles
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 red bell pepper, julienned
- 1 cup shredded carrot
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1 tbsp oil, for frying

## Steps

1. Mix together the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and sriracha.
2. Drain and rinse the shirataki noodles in a colander under running water for 1 minute. Set aside to dry in the colander until ready to use.
3. Heat the oil in a non-stick wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the garlic and fry for 1 minute.
4. Add the red bell pepper and carrot. Sauté for 3–4 minutes, until slightly softened.
5. Add the cabbage and cook another 2–3 minutes, until the veggies are tender.
6. Add the noodles along with the stir-fry sauce. Toss to coat and fry until the noodles are hot, then remove from heat. Season as needed and serve.

## Notes

- Add shredded chicken for extra protein.
