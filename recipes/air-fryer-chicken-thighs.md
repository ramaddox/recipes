# Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

Boneless chicken thighs with a brown-sugar spice rub, crisped in the air fryer.

## Ingredients

- 4 boneless chicken thighs
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 tsp brown sugar
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp parsley
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- 1/2 tsp onion powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper

## Steps

1. Preheat the air fryer to 400F.
2. Pat the chicken dry and drizzle with oil to coat.
3. Combine the brown sugar, paprika, parsley, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Rub over the chicken thighs.
4. Coat the basket with cooking spray, then air fry the thighs for 12-14 minutes, until they reach an internal temperature of 165F.
