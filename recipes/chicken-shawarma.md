# Chicken Shawarma

Juicy chicken thighs marinated in warm spices, garlic, and lemon, then roasted until crisp at the edges. Bold, smoky flavor made easy at home.

## Ingredients

- 2.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs
- 2–3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp plain yogurt
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1.5 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp dried thyme
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tbsp Aleppo pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/4 tsp cardamom
- 1/8 tsp nutmeg

## Steps

1. Combine the olive oil, yogurt, lemon juice, and all the seasonings, then rub the mixture into the chicken. Cover and marinate overnight.
2. Let the chicken rest at room temperature for 30 minutes before cooking.
3. Preheat the oven to 425°F.
4. Pack the chicken tightly into a loaf pan.
5. Roast for 45–55 minutes, until cooked through.
6. Cut into strips and serve over your choice of sides.

## Notes

- Keeps in the fridge for 4–5 days.
