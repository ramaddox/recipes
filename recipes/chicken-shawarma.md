# Chicken Shawarma

Juicy chicken thighs marinated in warm spices, garlic, and lemon, then roasted until crisp. Bold, smoky flavor made easy at home.

## Ingredients

- 2.5 lbs boneless chicken thighs
- 2-3 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp plain yogurt
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- 1 tbsp thyme
- 1.5 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika
- 1/2 tbsp Aleppo pepper
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp cumin
- 1/4 tsp cardamom
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon
- 1/8 tsp nutmeg

## Steps

1. Mix seasonings and rub in chicken, marinate overnight. 
2. Preheat oven to 425°F
3. Let chicken rest out for 30 minutes before cooking. 
4. Pack chicken tightly into bread pan. 
5. Cook for 45-55 minutes. 
6. Cut into strips and serve over your choice of sides. 
7. Holds 4-5 days in the fridge.
