# Peanut Butter Balls

No-bake peanut butter balls made with just a handful of wholesome ingredients—natural peanut butter, honey, and almond flour—dipped in dark chocolate. The perfect protein-rich snack to satisfy a sweet craving.

## Ingredients

- 250g natural peanut butter
- 75g honey
- 45g almond flour
- Pinch of sea salt
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate melts

## Steps

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter, honey, almond flour, and salt. Stir until a dough forms that is easy to handle and roll into balls (8 x 45g). You can add another tablespoon of almond flour to the mixture, if needed, to get the right texture for rolling.
2. Scoop the dough by tablespoons and roll the dough between your hands to make balls. Place the balls on a pan lined with parchment paper, then put them in the freezer to set until firm, about 20 minutes.
3. When the peanut butter balls feel firm to the touch, melt the chocolate.
4. Remove the peanut butter balls from the freezer and dip each one into the melted chocolate mixture for a chocolate base. Take a fork and scoop up remaining chocolate mixture, drizzling the chocolate over the peanut butter balls.
5. Return the balls to the parchment paper. The chocolate should start to harden right away, but you can place them in the fridge to completely set.
6. Serve cold. These should keep well in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks, in the freezer for up to 3 months.
