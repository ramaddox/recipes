# Peanut Butter Balls

No-bake peanut butter balls made with just a handful of wholesome ingredients — natural peanut butter, honey, and almond flour — dipped in dark chocolate. A protein-rich snack that satisfies a sweet craving.

## Ingredients

- 250g natural peanut butter
- 75g honey
- 45g almond flour, plus more as needed
- Pinch of sea salt
- 1/2 cup dark chocolate melts

## Steps

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the peanut butter, honey, almond flour, and salt until a dough forms that is easy to handle and roll. If needed, add another tablespoon of almond flour to reach the right texture.
2. Scoop the dough by the tablespoon and roll between your hands into balls (about 8 x 45g). Place on a parchment-lined pan and freeze until firm, about 20 minutes.
3. Once the balls feel firm to the touch, melt the chocolate.
4. Dip each ball into the melted chocolate for a chocolate base, then use a fork to drizzle the remaining chocolate over the tops.
5. Return the balls to the parchment paper. The chocolate should begin to harden right away; place them in the fridge to set completely.
6. Serve cold.

## Notes

- Keeps in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 2 weeks, or in the freezer for up to 3 months.
