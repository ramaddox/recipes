# Chicken Salad w/ Avocado

A simple, mayo-free chicken salad bound with mashed avocado, parsley, and lemon.

## Ingredients

- Shredded rotisserie chicken
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1/2 avocado, mashed
- 2 tsp parsley
- 1/2 lemon, juiced

## Steps

1. Stir together the cooled chicken, olive oil, mashed avocado, parsley, and lemon juice.
2. Keep refrigerated up to 2 days.
