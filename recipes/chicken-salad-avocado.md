# Chicken Salad with Avocado

A simple, mayo-free chicken salad bound with mashed avocado, fresh parsley, and lemon. Light, creamy, and ready in minutes.

## Ingredients

- Shredded rotisserie chicken
- 2 tsp olive oil
- 1/2 avocado, mashed
- 2 tsp fresh parsley
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

## Steps

1. Stir together the cooled chicken, olive oil, mashed avocado, parsley, and lemon juice until combined.
2. Serve, or keep refrigerated for up to 2 days.
