# Turkey Injection

A savory butter injection with chicken broth, Worcestershire, and warm poultry seasonings that infuses your Thanksgiving turkey from the inside out. The secret to juicy, deeply flavored meat in every bite.

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup chicken broth
- 2 tbsp butter
- 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp black pepper

## Steps

1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Simmer in saucepan over low heat.
3. Allow to cool, then inject.
