# Turkey Injection

A savory butter injection with chicken broth, Worcestershire, and warm poultry seasonings that flavors your turkey from the inside out. The secret to juicy, deeply seasoned meat.

## Ingredients

- 2/3 cup chicken broth
- 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- 2 tbsp butter
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1 tsp poultry seasoning
- 1 tsp thyme
- 1 tsp black pepper

## Steps

1. Combine all the ingredients in a saucepan.
2. Simmer over low heat until blended.
3. Let cool, then inject into the turkey.
