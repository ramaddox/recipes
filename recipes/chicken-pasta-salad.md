# Chicken & Pasta Salad

A chilled fusilli pasta salad with fresh veggies, salami, and rotisserie chicken in a lemon-herb dressing.

## Ingredients

- 1 rotisserie chicken
- 8 oz fusilli pasta
- 1/3 cup diced cucumber
- 1/3 cup red bell pepper
- 1 small can black olives, sliced
- 1/3 cup sliced salami
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp honey
- 1.4 oz olive oil
- Juice of 1/2 a lemon
- Dash of black pepper

## Steps

1. Cook the pasta according to package directions, until al dente. Drain, then run cold water over it to stop the cooking and cool it down.
2. While the pasta cooks, make the dressing: in a measuring cup or mason jar, combine the thyme, oregano, lemon juice, olive oil, honey, and black pepper. Shake or stir well.
3. Cut the veggies and salami and arrange in a large mixing bowl.
4. Add the cooled pasta to the bowl and stir in the dressing.
5. Cover with plastic wrap and chill in the fridge for at least one hour.
6. Serve topped with shredded rotisserie chicken.
