# Cabbage Rolls

Low-carb cabbage rolls stuffed with beef, sausage, cauliflower rice, and zucchini in tomato sauce.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb ground sausage
- 1 cup riced cauliflower
- 1 head of cabbage
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp pepper
- 20 oz low-carb tomato sauce

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 350F.
2. Bring a large stockpot of water to a boil.
3. Wash the head of cabbage and remove the leaves from the head.
4. Add the cabbage leaves to the boiling water and cook until tender and pliable, about 4-7 minutes depending on thickness. Use tongs to remove them and set aside on a paper-towel-lined plate to drain and cool.
5. While the cabbage boils, cook the riced cauliflower according to the bag.
6. Cook the ground beef and sausage in a large skillet until done, stirring regularly. Drain, return to the skillet, and stir in the salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
7. In a large bowl, mix the zucchini, cauliflower rice, and meat until well combined.
8. Spray the bottom of a 9x13 pan with non-stick cooking spray.
9. Pour a small amount of sauce on the bottom, just enough to cover it.
10. Fill each cabbage leaf with the mixture and wrap. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the top.
11. Bake at 350F for 40-50 minutes.
