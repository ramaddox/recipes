# Pork & Beef Meatballs

Oven-baked pork and beef meatballs served over rotini with pasta sauce and parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 3 large eggs, beaten
- 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
- Parsley
- 1/2 cup grated parmesan, plus more for garnish
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground pepper
- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 lb ground pork
- 16 oz rotini
- 24 oz pasta sauce

## Steps

1. Preheat oven to 450F. Line a large baking sheet with foil and coat with cooking spray.
2. Combine the eggs, panko, parsley, parmesan, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
3. Add the beef and pork. Mix gently with your hands until just combined (do not overmix).
4. Shape into meatballs (about 35) and place 1 inch apart on the baking sheet.
5. Bake until the meatballs reach an internal temperature of 165F, about 15 minutes.
6. Cook the pasta according to package directions.
7. Combine the pasta and pasta sauce.
8. Add the meatballs.
9. Garnish with parmesan, if desired.
