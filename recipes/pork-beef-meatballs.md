# Pork & Beef Meatballs

Tender oven-baked pork and beef meatballs served over rotini with pasta sauce and Parmesan. A weeknight favorite.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb lean ground beef
- 1 lb ground pork
- 3 large eggs, beaten
- 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan, plus more for garnish
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tbsp fresh parsley
- 1 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp ground black pepper
- 16 oz rotini
- 24 oz pasta sauce

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 450°F. Line a large baking sheet with foil and coat with cooking spray.
2. Combine the eggs, panko, parsley, Parmesan, garlic, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
3. Add the beef and pork. Mix gently with your hands until just combined; do not overmix.
4. Shape into meatballs (about 35) and place 1 inch apart on the baking sheet.
5. Bake until the meatballs reach an internal temperature of 165°F, about 15 minutes.
6. Meanwhile, cook the pasta according to the package directions.
7. Combine the pasta and pasta sauce, then add the meatballs.
8. Garnish with Parmesan and serve.
