# Cabbage Rolls

Low-carb cabbage rolls stuffed with a savory blend of beef, sausage, cauliflower rice, and zucchini, baked in tomato sauce. Hearty and satisfying without the grains.

## Ingredients

- 1/2 lb ground beef
- 1/2 lb ground sausage
- 1 cup riced cauliflower
- 1 head cabbage
- 1 zucchini, diced
- 1 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 20 oz low-carb tomato sauce

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Bring a large stockpot of water to a boil.
3. Wash the cabbage and remove the leaves from the head.
4. Add the cabbage leaves to the boiling water and cook until tender and pliable, about 4–7 minutes depending on thickness. Use tongs to remove them, then set aside on a paper-towel-lined plate to drain and cool.
5. While the cabbage boils, cook the riced cauliflower according to the package directions.
6. Cook the ground beef and sausage in a large skillet until browned, stirring regularly. Drain, return to the skillet, and stir in the salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
7. In a large bowl, mix the zucchini, cauliflower rice, and meat until well combined.
8. Spray the bottom of a 9x13 pan with cooking spray and spread a thin layer of tomato sauce to cover it.
9. Fill each cabbage leaf with the mixture, wrap, and arrange seam-side down in the pan. Pour the remaining tomato sauce over the top.
10. Bake for 40–50 minutes, then serve.
