# 3-2-1 Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone oven ribs using the 3-2-1 method: 3 hours uncovered, 2 hours wrapped, 1 hour sauced. Low, slow, and worth the wait.

## Ingredients

- 1 rack ribs
- Dijon mustard
- Rib rub
- Apple cider vinegar
- BBQ sauce of choice

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 225°F. Place a pan of water on the bottom rack.
2. Remove the membrane from the ribs.
3. Rub the rack with a light layer of Dijon mustard.
4. Dust with your rib rub and work it into the meat.
5. Roast for 3 hours, spritzing with apple cider vinegar occasionally wherever the ribs look dry.
6. Remove from the oven, spray the rack with apple cider vinegar, wrap in foil, and return to the oven for 2 hours.
7. Unwrap the rack and lather with the BBQ sauce of your choice.
8. Return to the oven for 1 hour (30–45 minutes for high-sugar sauces).
9. Remove from the oven, rest briefly, and serve.
