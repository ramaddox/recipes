# Green Bean Casserole

A comforting classic made easy: tender French-style green beans in a creamy sauce, topped with crispy French fried onions and baked until golden. Serve as a cozy side, or add shredded chicken to make it a meal.

## Ingredients

- 2 cans French-style green beans, drained
- 1 can cream of broccoli soup
- 1/2 cup milk
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 package French fried onions
- Salt and black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
2. Stir together the cream of broccoli soup, milk, soy sauce, green beans, and half of the French fried onions. Season with salt and pepper.
3. To make it a main dish, stir in 2–3 chicken tenders or 1 chicken breast, cooked and shredded.
4. Bake for 25 minutes, then stir the mixture.
5. Top with the remaining French fried onions and return to the oven for 5 minutes, until the onions are golden brown.
6. Serve warm.

## Notes

- Add a poultry protein, such as chicken or turkey, to take this from a side dish to a main dish.
