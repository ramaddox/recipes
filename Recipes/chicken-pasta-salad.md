# Chicken & Pasta Salad

A chilled fusilli pasta salad with crisp veggies, salami, and rotisserie chicken in a bright lemon-herb dressing. Refreshing and easy to make ahead.

## Ingredients

- 1 rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 8 oz fusilli pasta
- 1/3 cup cucumber, diced
- 1/3 cup red bell pepper, diced
- 1/3 cup salami, sliced
- 1 small can black olives, sliced
- 1/2 tsp dried thyme
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1 tsp honey
- 1.4 oz olive oil
- Juice of 1/2 lemon
- Black pepper, to taste

## Steps

1. Cook the pasta according to the package directions, until al dente. Drain, then run cold water over it to stop the cooking and cool it down.
2. While the pasta cooks, make the dressing: in a measuring cup or mason jar, combine the thyme, oregano, lemon juice, olive oil, honey, and black pepper. Shake or stir well.
3. Dice the veggies and salami and add them to a large mixing bowl.
4. Add the cooled pasta to the bowl and stir in the dressing.
5. Cover and chill in the fridge for at least 1 hour.
6. Serve topped with the shredded rotisserie chicken.
