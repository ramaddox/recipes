# Eggnog Pie

A no-bake eggnog cream pie on a cinnamon graham crust, with a homemade eggnog recipe included. Festive, creamy, and make-ahead friendly.

## Ingredients

**For the graham cracker crust**

- 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs, finely crushed
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 6 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon

**For the homemade eggnog**

- 5 eggs, yolks and whites separated
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 cups whole milk
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 4 cloves
- Pinch of cinnamon
- Pinch of nutmeg
- Splash of vanilla

**For the eggnog pie**

- 2 cups cold eggnog
- 5.1 oz box vanilla pudding
- 1 cup whipped cream, folded in
- Splash of dark spiced rum

## Steps

**Graham cracker crust**

1. Grease a 9-inch glass pie pan and set aside.
2. Mix all the crust ingredients and press evenly into the pan.

**Homemade eggnog**

1. Whisk together the egg yolks and sugar. Set aside.
2. Add the whole milk, heavy cream, cloves, and a pinch of cinnamon to a pot. Bring to scalding, not a boil.
3. Slowly whisk the milk mixture into the egg yolks and sugar to temper.
4. Return to the pot over low heat and whisk until thick.
5. Strain the mixture.
6. Fluff the egg whites with a hand mixer, adding a pinch of sugar.
7. Fold the egg whites into the milk mixture, adding a pinch of nutmeg and a splash of vanilla.
8. Let rest in the fridge for at least 4 hours.

**Eggnog pie**

1. Mix together the cold eggnog and the vanilla pudding, making sure there are no clumps.
2. Beat together the whipped cream, sugar, and a splash of vanilla.
3. Fold the whipped cream into the eggnog mixture, then add a splash of dark spiced rum.
4. Pour over the graham cracker crust, spreading evenly, and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
