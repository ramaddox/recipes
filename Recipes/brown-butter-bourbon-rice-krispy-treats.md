# Brown Butter Bourbon Rice Krispy Treats

Elevated rice krispy treats made with nutty brown butter and a splash of bourbon, finished with flaky salt. Rich, chewy, and a little grown-up.

## Ingredients

- 2 sticks salted butter
- 3 (12 oz) bags mini marshmallows, divided
- 1/4 cup bourbon
- 1 tbsp vanilla extract
- 1/4 tsp kosher salt
- 10 cups (one 12 oz box) rice cereal, such as Rice Krispies
- Flaky salt, to garnish

## Steps

1. Grease a 9x13 glass baking pan and set aside.
2. In a very large pot over medium-high heat, melt the butter, stirring constantly, until the milk solids at the bottom brown and the butter smells nutty and darkens in color, about 5 minutes. Do not leave it unattended.
3. As soon as the butter browns, add two bags of the marshmallows along with the bourbon, vanilla, and kosher salt. Cook for 5–6 minutes, stirring constantly, until golden, smooth, and glossy.
4. Turn off the heat, add the remaining bag of marshmallows, and stir just until melted. Add the rice cereal and quickly stir to coat. (If your pot isn't big enough, transfer to a large mixing bowl as soon as the mixture is ready.)
5. Transfer to the prepared pan and gently press into an even layer. If it's too sticky, coat your hands in butter or oil. Garnish with flaky salt.
6. Let cool, then cut into squares and serve.
